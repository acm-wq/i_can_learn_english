## Words when I'm learning
#### 🟦 - I learn / 🟥 - I don't know


- 🟦 describe - описывать
- 🟦 carefully - осторожно, внимательно
- 🟦 laugh /laf/ - смеяться
- 🟦 adult - взрослый
- 🟦 instead - вместо (яблоко а не лимон)
- 🟦 shout - кричать
- 🟦 expect - ожидать
- 🟦 behave /bihave/ - вести себя
- 🟦 during - во время
- 🟦 avoid - избегать
- 🟦 calm /kalm/ - спокойный
- 🟦 suppose - предполагать
- 🟦 concern - забота, беспокойство
- 🟦 appropriate - подходящий, уместный
- 🟦 punish - наказывать
- 🟦 shake - встряхивать (бутылка)
- 🟦 increase - увеличивать

— Why are you laughing so loudly when I'm trying to describe my day?

— Oh, I'm just trying to avoid boring adult stories, but instead, I was expecting to hear something more appropriate.

— Well, I tried to behave calmly and carefully, but I had to shout to increase attention!

— You know, it's not always appropriate when adults behave like that. They should care more about how they act during conversations.

— I think if I were an adult, I could have some fun too! Why not shake up the bottle and enjoy myself?

— I suppose you can do that, but be careful not to punish me for my funny moments!

— Hahaha! But if you don't laugh, I might start to concern myself!
