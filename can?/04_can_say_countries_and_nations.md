## I Can Say Countries and Nations

### 📚 Basic Questions and Answers:

- **Where are you from?**
- **Where do you come from?**

**Possible answers:**
- I come from ...
- I'm ...
- I speak ...

---

### 🌍 Countries and Nationals

**For Europe**: (Mostly -ish suffix)
- Britain → British
- Spain → Spanish
- Denmark → Danish
- Ireland → Irish
- Sweden → Swedish

**Exceptions:**
- Germany → German
- France → French
- Greece → Greek
- Italy → Italian
- Russia → Russian

**For America**: (Mostly -an/ian suffix)
- America → American
- Canada → Canadian
- Brazil → Brazilian

**Exceptions:**
- Mexico → Mexican


**For Asia**: (Mostly -ese suffix)
- China → Chinese
- Japan → Japanese
- Vietnam → Vietnamese

**Exceptions:**
- India → Indian
- Israel → Israeli

### 🗺️ Key Words for Directions:

- **North**
- **South**
- **West**
- **East**

- **Northwest (NW)**
- **Northeast (NE)**
- **Southwest (SW)**
- **Southeast (SE)**

---

- Moscow is in the west of Russia.
- Japan is to the east of China.
- Poland is to the west of India.
