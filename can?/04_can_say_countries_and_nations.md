## I can say countries and nations?

### Basic questions and answer:

Where are you from?
Where do you come from?

I come from ...
I'm ...
I speak ...

### Countries and nationals

For **Europe**: - ish ex: 
```
Britain → British
Spain → Spanish
Denmark → Danish
Ireland → Irish
Sweden → Swedish

Exceptions
Germany → German
France → French
Greece → Greek
Italy → Italian
Russia → Russian
```

For **America**: -an/ian

```
America → American
Canada → Canadian
Brazil → Brazilian

Exceptions
Mexico → Mexican
```

For **Asia**: -ese
```
China → Chinese
Japan → Japanese
Vietnam → Vietnamese

Exceptions
India → Indian
Israel → Israeli
```

### Key words

- North
- South
- West
- East

- Northwest (NW)
- Northeast (NE)
- Southwest (SW)
- Southeast (SE)

Exs:

Moscow is in the west of Russia.

Japan is to the east of China.

Poland is to the west of India.

Germany is in central Europe.
