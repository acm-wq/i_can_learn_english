## I Can Use English Language Words

### 📚 Parsing the Sentence

**Sentence 1: The small dog barked loudly at night.**

- **A sentence** - [ The ]
- **Adjective** - [ Small ]
- **Noun** - [ Dog ]
- **Verb** - [ Barked ]
- **Adverb** - [ Loudly ]
- **Preposition** - [ At ]
- **Definite article** - [ The ]
- **Noun** - [ Night ]

---

**Sentence 2: A young girl ran quickly across the street.**

- **A sentence** - [ A ]
- **Adjective** - [ Young ]
- **Noun** - [ Girl ]
- **Verb** - [ Ran ]
- **Adverb** - [ Quickly ]
- **Preposition** - [ Across ]
- **Definite article** - [ The ]
- **Noun** - [ Street ]

---

**Sentence 3: She talked to a tall man and three guys. "Hello," she said.**

- **Pronoun** - [ She ]
- **Singular noun** - [ Man ]
- **Plural noun** - [ Guys ]
- **Phrase** - [ Hello ]
- **Verb** - [ Talked ]
- **Preposition** - [ To ]
- **Article** - [ A ]
- **Adjective** - [ Tall ]
- **Conjunction** - [ And ]
- **Number** - [ Three ]
- **Quotation/Direct Speech** - [ "Hello," ]
