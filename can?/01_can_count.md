### 📖 Cardinal vs. Ordinal Numbers

- **Cardinal Numbers** (How many):  
  - **one**, **two**, **three**, etc.
  - Example:  
    - I have **two** apples.  
    - There are **five** books on the table.

- **Ordinal Numbers** (Position or order):  
  - **first**, **second**, **third**, etc.
  - Example:  
    - This is my **first** lesson.  
    - He came in **second** place in the race.

**Ordinal Numbers Examples:**
- **First** – first
- **Second** – second
- **Third** – third
- **Fourth** – fourth
- **Fifth** – fifth
- **Sixth** – sixth
- **Eleventh** – eleventh
- **Twentieth** – twentieth
- **Thirtieth** – thirtieth
- **Hundredth** – hundredth

### 🧮 Mathematical Expressions

When I want to say:

- **5 - 3 * 20**, I say: **Five minus three times twenty.**
- **9 / 12 * (3 + 2)**, I say: **Nine divided by twelve times the sum of three and two.**
- **9^12**, I say: **Nine to the power of twelve.**
- **√25**, I say: **The square root of twenty-five.**
