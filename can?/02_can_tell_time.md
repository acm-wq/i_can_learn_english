## I Can Tell the Time

### ⏰ Asking the Time

When I want to ask the time, I'll say:
- **What time is it? ⏰**
- **Excuse me, what time is it now?**

---

### 🕐 Telling the Time

- 🕐 It's one o'clock.
- 🕞 It's half past three.
- 🕣 It's twenty to nine.
- 🕥 It's ten thirty.
- 🕘 It's nine o'clock.
- 🕦 It's half past eleven.
- 🕑 It's two o'clock.
- 🕤 It's quarter to ten.

For example, if it's 9:30 AM, we say: "It's half past nine in the morning."

For example, if it's 6:40 PM, we say: "It's twenty to seven in the evening."

---

### 🌅 Key Words for Time of Day:

- **🌅 6:00 AM – 11:59 AM** Morning
- **🍽️ 1:00 PM – 2:00 PM** After lunch
- **🌞 12:00 PM – 5:59 PM** In the afternoon
- **🌆 6:00 PM – 9:59 PM** In the evening
- **⏰ 3:59 AM** – Nearly/almost four
- **⏰ 4:02 AM** – Just after four
- **🍛 8:00 PM – 9:30 PM** After dinner
- **🌙 12:00 AM** – Midnight
- **🌙 10:00 PM – 4:59 AM** At night
